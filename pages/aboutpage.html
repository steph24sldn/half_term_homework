About Us Welcome to Jolistedo! At Jolistedo, we believe that every drink tells a story. Founded in 2024 by Stephan Noticia, our mission is to connect people with exceptional beverages that enhance their moments, whether big or small. Our Mission Our mission is simple: to provide a curated selection of the finest drinks, from craft beers and exquisite wines to refreshing non-alcoholic options. We strive to create a welcoming space where everyone can discover new flavors, learn about their favorite beverages, and celebrate life’s special occasions. Our Selection At Jolistedo, we take pride in our diverse range of products. Our team travels extensively to source drinks from local artisans and renowned brands, ensuring that each bottle reflects our commitment to quality and craftsmanship. Whether you’re looking for a bold red wine to pair with dinner, a craft beer for a casual gathering, or a unique non-alcoholic beverage to enjoy at any time, we have something for every palate. Our Values Quality: We believe in offering only the best. Each product in our selection undergoes careful vetting to ensure it meets our high standards. Community: We support local producers and aim to foster a sense of community by connecting drink enthusiasts with the artisans behind their favorite beverages. Education: We’re dedicated to sharing our knowledge. Through tastings, workshops, and informative content, we empower our customers to make informed choices and expand their understanding of drinks. Our Journey Founded in 2024 by Stephan Noticia, Jolistedo began as a small passion project. Over the years, we have grown into a beloved destination for drink enthusiasts, thanks to the support of our loyal customers and partners. Our team is made up of passionate individuals who are not only knowledgeable about drinks but also dedicated to providing exceptional customer service. Join Us At Jolistedo, we invite you to explore the world of drinks with us. Whether you’re a connoisseur or just starting your journey, we’re here to help you discover new favorites, learn about the art of drink-making, and create lasting memories. Connect with Us We love hearing from our customers! If you have any questions, feedback, or just want to share your drink experiences, feel free to reach out. You can also follow us on social media for the latest updates, promotions, and events. Thank you for choosing Jolistedo. We look forward to being a part of your drink journey!
